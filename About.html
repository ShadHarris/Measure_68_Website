Why is the Fee Needed?

Athletics at UCSC is currently running on a defecit. Continuing on the way we are, athletics will no longer be a part of this university in years to come. This fee is necessary to keep athletics on this campus while maintaining a price comparabely lower than that of any other UC or CSU school. Athletics provides students with a way to relieve stress, find emotional stability, feel good about themselves, and much more. It is important for promoting physical and mental well being and is something that should not be overlooked as insignificant.

Main Points

  • The goal of this measure is to create long-term continuous support for intercollegiate athletics.
  • Enact a new fee of $38.50 per student, per quarter. Fee will generate approximately $2,000,000 per year.
  • Passing of this fee is required to maintain an intercollegiate athletics program (NCAA Division-III) at UCSC.
  • 59% of the fee will go to Intercollegiate Athletics
  • 8% will generate approximately $160,000 to provide access for athletics-related activities and services for students who meet EOP criteria (to be allocated by EOP).
  • EOP students currently represent approximately 40% of the student population.
  • The fee begins Fall Quarter 2017 with an end date of Spring Quarter 2042.
